Dorito Pizza Pops with a Monster Cheez-It Reduction

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 8, hopefully

Dough

Ingredients Quantity
Doritos, flavour of choice 165 grams, coarsely chopped
Flour 440 grams
Cold water 355 millilitres
Sugar 30 grams
Yeast 15 grams
Salt 15 grams

Filling

Ingredients Quantity
Extra-virgin olive oil 15 millilitres
Garlic 25 grams, roasted and minced
Shallots 1, brunoised
San Marzano tomatoes 7, peeled and large diced
Tomato Paste 15 grams
Mexican oregano 25 grams
Anise seed 7 grams
Salt and pepper To taste
Camembert de Normandie 80 grams, small diced
Jamon Iberico de bellota 50 grams, chiffonaded

Reduction

Ingredients Quantity
Monster, original is best 2 cans
Cheez-Its 250 grams

Step 1: To begin the dough, we need to proof the yeast. Place the water, sugar, and yeast into a bowl for it to activate and consume the sugars. Leave it for a handful of minutes for the process to continue.

Step 2: Add the flour, Doritos, and salt to the water. Mix to a smooth texture. This is so much easier with a stand mixer, but you could just go for it if you truly wanted. Let rise for 10 minutes, allowing the dough to expand. Refrigerate after until ready to prepare.

Step 4:Prepare the dough by dividing it into palm sized balls. Flatten the dough to about a 6 millimetre thickness and form into a circle. Spread the tomato sauce mixture along one half of the dough, leaving about a 20 millimetre gap from the edge. When full, take the half without filling and drape it over top, fully enveloping the other half. Press down on the edges to crimp the dough. This can also be done with a fork. Cut a couple vents into the Pizza Pop and brush with some olive oil.

Step 6: Place the Pizza Pops onto the pizza stone. These will need about 15-20 minutes. In the meantime, it's time to start the reduction. Pour the Monster into a small saucepot and bring to a boil. Add the Cheez-Its to the boiling Monster and reduce the mixture. If necessary, you can add a corn starch slurry, but be careful about overthickening it. When at the desired consistency, strain through a fine sieve and let cool.

Step 7: Pull them out when cooked fully and let them cool for 5 minutes before eating. Drizzle the reduction onto the Pizza Pops or use it as a dip.

Icy Rebound Sandwiches

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 24
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Have the ice cream sit in room temperature for about 5 minutes, until the ice cream is malleable but not melted.
